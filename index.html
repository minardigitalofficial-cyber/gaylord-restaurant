About

THE GAYLORD RESTAURANT

In the West, the Indian subcontinent often evokes images of heritage, vibrant flavours, and cultural depth. India has shaped British dining for generations, yet many still assume Indian food is simply a basic “curry” created with generic spices. At GAYLORD (73 Queens Rd, Weybridge KT13 9UQ, United Kingdom) we are here to break that misconception.

We believe Indian cuisine is a craft. Built on tradition, patience, and respect. Our cooking reflects the genuine approach found in real Indian homes: carefully prepared dishes, honest ingredients, balanced seasoning, and authentic methods passed down through time.

We take pride in serving true Indian food to the people of Weybridge, with integrity and flavour at the heart of everything. At GAYLORD, we don’t just serve meals — we carry forward a legacy of authentic Indian culinary tradition.
